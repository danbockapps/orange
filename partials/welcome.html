Welcome to Be Healthy Now

Be Healthy Now, Alamance County's free 10-week fitness program, is making changes for the better so that YOU can also take steps to imrove your health and wellness. Take a look at just a few of the exciting changes:

  • Improved and easier-to-use points system
  • Earn points for eating fruits and veggies
  • Yoga in the park one afternoon a week
  • Free passes to several participating gyms
  • Nutrition and cooking classes for the whole family
  • "Try Something New" Mondays (kayaking, karate, line dancing, etc.)

What is Be Healthy Now?

A 10-week Wellness Challenge to encourage Alamance County residents of ALL ages to exercise, eat well and enjoy healthy lives. Every Be Healthy Now activity -- whether a relaxing group hike, an energizing fitness class, or a tasty cooking demonstration -- is completely free of charge to registered Challengers. Plus - our sponsors and partners offer discounts on healthy products and services to Be Healthy Now Challengers.

You can form your own Be Healthy Now team -- with as few as 2 and as many as 10 members - from among your friends, family, co-workers, faith groups, and classmates. Be Healthy Now team members support each other to achieve their goals.

The Challenge kicks-off April 11 and continues through June 19th. Register your team no later than April 11th.

Challengers update their progress on-line as they work towards their individual and team goals. At the end of the competition, the teams that complete their goals will be entered into a drawing for four CASH prizes of $250 each!

Have questions? Call the Alamance Regional Live Well Line at 336-586-4000 for more information.

Register yourself and your team

  1. Gather your team -- as few as 2 and as many as 10 members per team. Choose a name for your team. (Some of our favorites from 2014 were #SkinnyJeans and Mission ThinPossible.)
  2. Choose a team captain -- this person will register first.
  3. The captain will be emailed a team code; then just forward the email to each of your team members. They'll click and register right from the email, whether on a PC, tablet, phone or other mobile device. You're Challengers now!

Join our group on Facebook to earn extra points for your team and connect with other Challengers.

The last day to register is Saturday, April 11, 2015.
